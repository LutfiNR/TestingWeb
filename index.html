FITE FOOTBALL ACADEMY

ABOUT US

FITE Football Academy, we're dedicated to nurturing young football talent. Established in 2008, our vision is to create an environment that supports player development in football. We offer high-quality coaching, tailored training programs, and a commitment to character development. Our coaching staff is experienced, dedicated, and passionate about helping players reach their full potential. Join us to be part of a community where passion for football meets skill development. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, FITE Football Academy is the place where your football journey begins.

OUR HISTORY
2008

Founded in 2008, FA FITE has a rich history of nurturing football talent. Since our inception, we've been committed to developing young players, making our mark in the world of football. Our journey is a testament to the passion and dedication that drives us forward in shaping the future of the sport.



2012

Winning the National Youth Championship in 2012 and producing over 20 players who have successfully reached the professional level in football. We have also received recognition from the Regional Football Association as one of the leading academies in player development.



2013

Over the years, FA FITE has seen substantial growth. In 2013, we introduced top-notch training facilities with international-standard fields and a fitness center. We've expanded our programs, including youth player initiatives and adult fitness programs, and established a new branch in Konoha in 2017 to cater to a diverse player base.
